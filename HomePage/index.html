About


AgroSavvy is a Web Application that can help our beloved farmers to give them a great and wondeful recommendation using AgroSavvy


AgroSavvy is a platform focused on agriculture, designed to support key stakeholders in the agricultural sector such as farmers, local government officials (Barangay officials), and the Department of Agriculture. Its primary objective is to facilitate efficient communication, coordination, and resource management among these groups.

Key Features

Farmer Support: Provides tools and resources for farmers to improve their agricultural practices, access vital information.

Barangay Officials: Helps local government units (Barangay officials) manage agricultural activities within their areas, promote local farming initiatives, and ensure effective communication between farmers and government agencies.

Department of Agriculture: Serves as a bridge between the Department of Agriculture and local stakeholders, allowing for better distribution of government programs, subsidies, and information to farmers and local officials.

The AgroSavvy platform offers a user-friendly interface where each user type (farmer, Barangay official, Department of Agriculture representative) can register and access tailored services, making the process of agricultural management and support more efficient.

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to empower farmers, local government officials, and agricultural agencies with innovative tools and technology that streamline communication, enhance agricultural practices, and promote sustainable farming, ultimately improving food security and rural livelihoods."


VISION: Our vision is to be the leading digital platform revolutionizing agriculture by fostering collaboration, efficiency, and innovation in farming communities, ensuring a sustainable and prosperous future for all stakeholders in the agricultural ecosystem."
